Basketball

Basketball has been a crucial part of my life and is my favorite hobby. So, I think it deserves a dedicated section! Personally, I think it is the best sport in the world and provides a fun way of staying active. I've been told by friends and family that I play like Paul George. However, not only do I like playing baskebtall, but I also like watching it. I always try to watch every NBA game since it's amazing to see how good professionals are. When it comes to my favorite players, I have way too many as each of them have a unique skillset and playstyle. I hope to keep playing basketball for as long as my body can handle it, but even when the time comes when I have to put down the ball for good, at least I'll be able to watch NBA games!
